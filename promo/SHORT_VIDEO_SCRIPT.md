# AiAutomationBot — 30-Second Video Script

## Hook (0–5s)

**[Screen: terminal window]**
Narrator (upbeat, direct): "What if your online store had an AI support agent — set up in under 10 minutes, with no database?"

**[Text overlay: "No database. No cloud. Just a config file."]**

---

## Show (5–20s)

**[Screen: VS Code, `config/business.json` open]**
Narrator: "You fill in your products, prices, and store hours here."

**[Screen: switches to browser at `localhost:3000`]**
Customer types: "Do you ship internationally?"
Bot replies instantly with the store's policy from the config.

**[Screen: customer types "I want to order"]**
Bot: "Sure! What's your name and email?"
Customer fills it in. Bot confirms.

**[Screen: switches to `localhost:3000/admin`]**
Narrator: "And the lead shows up here — in your admin dashboard — instantly."
Dashboard shows lead row with name, email, timestamp. Sidebar shows conversation history and analytics chart.

---

## Close (20–30s)

**[Screen: GitHub repo page]**
Narrator: "Open source. MIT license. npm install and you're live."

**[Text overlay: repo URL]**
Narrator: "Link in bio — go build it."

**[End card with repo URL and "Star it on GitHub"]**
